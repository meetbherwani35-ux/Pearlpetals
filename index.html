Our Story

Crafting Beauty from Nature's Finest Treasures

Pearl Petals was born from a love of nature and the art of jewelry making. Founded in 2013, we started as a small studio where every piece was — and still is — handcrafted with intention and care.

We source the finest freshwater pearls and ethically mined gemstones, transforming them into wearable art that celebrates the beauty of the natural world. Each design draws inspiration from botanicals, ocean life, and the quiet elegance of a garden in bloom.
